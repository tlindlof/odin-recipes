Finnish Summer Soup

Description

This is a recipe for Finnish Summer Soup. Despite its name it is consumed all year round in Finland. It can also be heard called by its other names:

  • Winter Soup
  • Spring Soup
  • Fall Soup
  • Copious amounts of vodka

    • Ingredients

    • A large bowl of your choosing
    • 1 l Of cheap vodka
    • 1 Lingonberry

    Steps

    1. Add vodka to bowl. Yes all of it.
    2. Add lingonberry. Stir for one minute.
    3. Remove lingonberry.
    4. Enyoy the soup.

    If you want to really impress at the family function, you can leave the berry in the vodka after stirring for up to two minutes.
