Disaster

Description

This is a recipe for disaster. It is commonplace in the lives of humans. First invented by nature, it is now usually cooked up by humanity themselves on the daily. This recipe will focus on human made disaster.

Altough this is not everyones favourite, there is often that little something in disaster that you cannot get from anywhere else. That little something is anger.

Ingredients

  • Power over potential danger
  • Too much greed
  • 0 fucks
  • 1 Heap irresponsibility
  • Pre written excuse
  • (Optional) False promises of change for the better

Steps

  1. Take your potential danger and use it to satisfy your greed.
  2. When your potential danger starts to look like a realized danger give all your fucks
  3. To this, add your irresponibility and after a while you will have a disaster.
  4. If your disaster is not recieved well, remember to pull out your pre written excuse. If the recipiants are still a bit salty, you can add the optional promise that tis will never happen again.
