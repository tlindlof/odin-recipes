Odins Lasagna

Description

This is the renowned lasagna of Odin himself. A mere bite of this pasta dish is enough to get you wearing an eyepatch and start talking to ravens

Some of the ingredients can be hard to find. I allways go grocery shopping in the Aldi in west Joutunheimen, they allways carry the things i need.

Ingredients

  • 300g Pasta sheets
  • 500g Ground goat
  • 4 tbsp Tomato Puree
  • 1,2kg Crushed tomatoes (Canned)
  • 1 Onion (Small)
  • 2 Lingon berries

Steps

  1. Skin and grind the goat with the medium corse setting on your goat grinder
  2. Peel one (1) small onion and dice it
  3. Fry the onion until translucent as the Bifrost
  4. Add the ground up goat and fry until browned
  5. Add one lingonberry and look at it.
  6. Realize that lingonberry in a lasagna is lunacy. Remove the lingonberry!
  7. Add the rest of the ingredients. Let cook for a time eqivalent to three or four powernaps (Depending how long your powernaps usually are)
  8. Taste and adjust seasoning realize that you added the second lingonberry in step 7. Your day is now ruined.
